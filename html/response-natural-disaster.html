NaturalDisaster

Floods

Overflow of water onto normally dry land, often caused by heavy rainfall, storm surges, or dam failures.

Earthquakes

Sudden shaking of the ground caused by movements along tectonic plates.

Hurricanes, Cyclones, Typhoons

Powerful tropical storms with strong winds and heavy rainfall.

Tornadoes

Violently rotating columns of air extending from thunderstorms to the ground.

Wildfires

Uncontrolled fires that rapidly spread across vegetation and forests.

Landslides

Movement of rock, soil, and debris down a slope
